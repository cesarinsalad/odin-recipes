Brownies

Description

Brownies are the best dessert ever. They are soft, chewy, and delicious. They are perfect for any occasion.

Ingredients

  • 1 Cup of Butter
  • 1 Cup of Sugar
  • 1 Cup of Brown Sugar
  • 1 Teaspoon of Salt
  • 3 Eggs
  • 1 Teaspoon of Vanilla Extract
  • 1 Cup of Cocoa Powder
  • 200g Cup of Chocolate
  • 1 Teaspoon of Coffee
  • 1 Cup of Flour

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (180°C).
  2. Brown the butter in a large saucepan.
  3. Once browned, melt the chocolate inside the butter and add the cocoa powder and coffee. Let it rest.
  4. In a large bowl, add the sugars, salt and eggs. Beat until smooth.
  5. Once the butter and chocolate mixed has rested. Add it to the bowl and beat it until everything is mixed.
  6. Add the flour.
  7. Grease a pan and use a parchment paper-lined baking sheet to avoid the brownie sticking to the pan.
  8. Bake for 30 to 35 minutes, depending on how gooey you prefer it.
  9. Let it rest until the pan chills.
  10. Enjoy! :)
