Brookies

Description

Brookies are the best of both worlds. They are a combination of brownies and cookies. They are soft, chewy, and delicious. They are perfect for any occasion.

Ingredients for the Brownie

  • 1/2 Cup of Butter
  • 1/2 Cup of Sugar
  • 1/2 Cup of Brown Sugar
  • 1/2 Teaspoon of Salt
  • 2 Eggs
  • 1/2 Teaspoon of Vanilla Extract
  • 1/3 Cup of Cocoa Powder
  • 100g Cup of Chocolate
  • 1/2 Teaspoon of Coffee
  • 1/2 Cup of Flour

Ingredients for the Cookie

  • 1/2 Cup of Butter
  • 1/2 Cup of Sugar
  • 3/4 Cup of Brown Sugar
  • 1/2 Teaspoon of Salt
  • 1 Eggs
  • 1 Teaspoon of Vanilla Extract
  • 1 1/4 Cups of Flour
  • 1/2 Teaspoon of Baking Soda
  • 100g of Chocolate Chunks

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (180°C).
  2. For the brownie, brown the butter in a large saucepan.
  3. Once browned, melt the chocolate inside the butter and add the cocoa powder and coffee. Let it rest.
  4. In a large bowl, add the sugars, salt and eggs. Beat until smooth.
  5. Once the butter and chocolate mixed has rested. Add it to the bowl and beat it until everything is mixed.
  6. Add the flour.
  7. For the cookie, in a large bowl, whisk together the sugars, salt, and butter until combined.
  8. Whisk in the egg and vanilla until smooth.
  9. Sift in the flour and baking soda, then fold the mixture with a spatula.
  10. Fold in the chocolate chunks.
  11. Grease a pan and use a parchment paper-lined baking sheet to avoid the brookie sticking to the pan.
  12. Place the brownie dough on the bottom of the pan and the cookie dough on top of the brownie dough.
  13. Bake for 20 minutes covered in aluminium foil foil.
  14. Bake for another 20 minutes uncovered.
  15. Let it rest until the pan chills.
  16. Enjoy! :)
