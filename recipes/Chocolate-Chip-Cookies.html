Chocolate Chip Cookies

Description

Chocolate chip cookies are the best cookies ever. They are soft, chewy, and delicious. They are perfect for any occasion.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 Cup of Sugar
  • 3/4 Cup of Brown Sugar
  • 1 Teaspoon of Salt
  • 1/2 Cup of Butter
  • 1 Eggs
  • 1 Teaspoon of Vanilla Extract
  • 1 1/4 Cups of Flour
  • 1/2 Teaspoon of Baking Soda
  • 200g of Chocolate Chunks

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (180°C).
  2. In a large bowl, whisk together the sugars, salt, and butter until combined.
  3. Whisk in the egg and vanilla until smooth.
  4. Sift in the flour and baking soda, then fold the mixture with a spatula.
  5. Fold in the chocolate chunks.
  6. Scoop the dough into balls and place them on a baking sheet.
  7. Bake for 12 minutes, or until the edges have started to barely brown.
  8. Cool completely before serving.
  9. Enjoy! :)
